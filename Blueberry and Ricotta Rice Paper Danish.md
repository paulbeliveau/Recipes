title: Blueberry and Ricotta RIce Paper Danish
last made: 2026-06-23
times made: 1

---
Whisk the @egg{1} in a shallow bowl. Lightly dip each @rice paper sheet{3} into the egg wash until pliable. Stack on top of each other on a lightly oiled surface.

In a small bowl mix together @ricotta cheese{1/4%cup} and @cream cheese{2%tbsp} until smooth.

Spread the ricotta mixture into the center of the stacked rice paper, leaving a 1 in border. Scatter @blueberries{1/3%cup} evenly over the filling.

Fold each side of rice paper inwards to form a square. Brush top lightly with remaining egg wash and sprinkle with @sesame seeds{2%tbsp}.

Transfer to a parchment lined baking sheet and bake at 400F for ~{10} minutes or until golden and crispy.

Drizzle with @honey{}(optional) and serve warm.
