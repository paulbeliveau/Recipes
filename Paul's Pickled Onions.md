Combine @apple cider vinegar{1 part}, @soy sauce{1 part}, and @sugar{1 part} in a #saucepan{} to make a brine.

Heat over medium high heat until it boils.

Slice @sweet onion{} and crush @garlic cloves{}  and add to #mason jar{}.

Pour hot brine into mason jar.

Refrigerate at least ~overnight{8%hours}.
